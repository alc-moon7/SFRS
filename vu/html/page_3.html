Varendra International Journal for Interdisciplinary Research (VIJIR)

ISSN: 3078-7017 (Online), 3078-7009 (Print) DOI: 10.642961/xvar
Publisher: Center for Interdisciplinary Research, Varendra University, Bangladesh URL: https://vu.edu.bd/journal

Varendra International Journal for Interdisciplinary Research (VIJIR) is a peer reviewed high quality interdisciplinary journal of international standard. This journal shall welcome papers, both theoretical and applied, from all disciplines which are interdisciplinary in nature, maintain the international standard and give extra emphasis on action research taking place in the Varendra region of Bangladesh. VIJIR shall encourage researchers to submit their articles focused on new areas and frontiers scholarly studies in the following potential topics include, but are not limited to Engineering, Technology, Pure and Applied Science, Business, Law, Social Science, Arts, History, and Literature. VIJIR shall contain three sections:

  • â€¢ Section: Arts and Social Science
  • â€¢ Section: Business and Law
  • â€¢ Section: Science, Engineering & Technology

Each section should follow the standard template of its own discipline. The details of it shall be available in the 'Instruction for Authors' section of VIJIR. The publishers reserve the right to add new sections in to this journal depending on the current demand. Article published in this journal includes new research, reviews, letters, brief communication, case studies, and article of professional interest. VIJIR shall be an annual journal, but the publishers reserve the right to change the status of the journal or to publish special issues in honor of eminent scholars if required. Invited papers from distinguished scholars on pre-selected topics may be published. Each issue shall be forwarded by an Editorial note made by the Editor-in-Chief. VIJIR shall be an open access journal and each issue of this journal should be available at the website https://vu.edu.bd/journal . VIJIR shall have a printed version of it. Publication of each article is free of charge. However, to meet the increasing costs of printing and postage, this journal shall charge a token amount as the subscription fee for a hard copy of this journal. The details of it shall be available in the 'Subscription' section of VIJIR.
