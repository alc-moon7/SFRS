Message from Career Center

Think you know what the Career Center does? Think again! We offer a full range of services to help you with whatever plans you have for the future. Whether it’s a career, a part-time job, an internship, graduate study, a fellowship or scholarship, a skill development and/or training program, we are here for you. We can help you with everything from making career and/or taking major decisions to writing a resume to connecting with key employers and alumni. As our tagline says, Look at the Future, Learn from the Past . So whatever you want to do and wherever you want to go, you belong here!

The Career Center services and programs focus on our mission to connect all of our partners for the purpose of helping students, integrate their education, self-awareness, and experiences to empower a lifetime of career achievement and personal satisfaction. Wonder how it all works? Take a look at our unique process, and when you’re ready to get started, come by and visit us.

· Explore Your Options

· Develop Your Skills

· Build Experience

· Make the Transition

We are here to provide you all kind of services. Connect with people who have vast experiences, find a career that best fits your personality. Please visit us soon: it’s never too early– or too late– to get started on your amazing future! And while you’re here on our website, check out the latest notices for the present and upcoming events.

We are passionate about education dedicated to providing high-quality resources for learners from all backgrounds.
